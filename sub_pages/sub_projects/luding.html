Mass movements after earthquake

Coseismic landslides after the 2022-09-05 Luding earthquake.

Why shall we care?
After earthquakes, triggered coseismic landslides often lead to large accumulations of sediment on slopes and in riverbeds. Understanding how these mass movements evolve and how sediments are delivered over weeks to months remains a major challenge.
What I want to do?
This project aims to combine remote sensing and seismic instrumentation to investigate sediment transport dynamics in Wandong catchment.
