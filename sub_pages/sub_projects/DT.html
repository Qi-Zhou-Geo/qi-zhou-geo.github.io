Digital twins for natural hazards

Sediment deposited on a check dam in Ilgraten.

Why shall we care?
Natural hazard processes are highly complex, while human settlements remain vulnerable. Digital twin (DT) approaches provide a safe way to explore these processes, offering advanced modeling without disturbing the real world.
What I want to do?
This project aims to design a framework for digital twins of natural hazards at the catchment scale by integrating machine learning with numerical models.
