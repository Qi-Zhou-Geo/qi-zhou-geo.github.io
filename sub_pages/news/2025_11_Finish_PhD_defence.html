PhD defence

Celebrating the man who earned his ticket to academia.

When and Where?
2025-11-24, Golm, Germany
What I have done?
After submitting my PhD thesis at 13 June, I waited about six months before defending it. My dissertation reviewers (Prof. Dr. Jean Braun, Prof. Dr. Jordan Aaron, Dr. Kate Allstadt), examination committee (Prof. Dr. Niels Hovius, Prof. Dr. Fabrice Cotton, Prof. Dr. Oliver Korup), and supervisors (Principal Supervisor: Prof. Dr. Jean Braun, Second Supervisor Dr. Jens M. Turowski, Co-Supervisor Dr. Hui Tang, Dr. Michael Dietze), friends, colleagues, and partner joined me for the PhD defense.

I successfully passed with magna cum laude (Latin academic honor meaning “with great praise”), an accolade typically awarded to graduates in the top 10–15% of their class.

Thanks for all my funding angency, friends and colleagues, collaborators and supervisors.
