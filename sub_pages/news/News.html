Geo-INQUIRE Sicily Summer School 2025

Sediment deposited on a check dam in Ilgraten.

When and Where?
2025-10-16 to 2025-10-23, Catania, Italy
What I have done?
Thanks to the INGV and INFN for organizing the Geo-INQUIRE Sicily Summer School 2025.
During the program, we explored theoretical aspects of Distributed Acoustic Sensing (DAS) and other advanced monitoring systems for geohazards around Mount Etna.
I am excited to further apply DAS technologies to my study and mitigation of natural hazards.
